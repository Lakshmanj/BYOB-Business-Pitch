FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial?
Yes — 14 days, no credit card required. After that, you'll need a paid plan to keep your site live. If you don't upgrade, your site goes offline and your content is held in our system for 30 days before deletion.
Can I fully customise my site's design and functionality?
You can customise within the bounds of our template library — colours, fonts, and layout blocks. Custom CSS is available on Pro+, but JavaScript injection and backend logic require our Enterprise plan or a separate custom development engagement. Our AI generates designs based on your inputs, though results may look similar to other sites in your industry.
Can I export or move my site to another platform?
Content can be exported as a PDF summary on Pro+ plans. Full code export, database migration, and asset portability are not supported. Moving to a custom-built platform typically requires rebuilding your site from scratch — which is why many of our Enterprise customers engage a web development agency for the transition.
Does TechFlow help with digital strategy or UX research?
TechFlow builds what you describe. We don't conduct user research, competitive audits, or long-term KPI planning — the platform responds to your inputs, not your business goals. For strategic guidance, we recommend working with a dedicated digital agency.
What happens if I need a feature that isn't on the platform?
You can submit a feature request — our team reviews these quarterly. If a feature is critical to your business and isn't on our roadmap, your options are limited: workarounds using third-party add-ons (at additional cost), or migrating to a custom-built solution.
